## Overview

Arvind Gaba provides a centralized hub for your project documentation. You create, organize, and collaborate on docs without the hassle of scattered files or complex tools. Streamline your workflow with intuitive features that scale from solo projects to team environments.

This space supports Markdown, embeds, and custom layouts, making it ideal for developers, product managers, and teams. Start documenting immediately and keep everything versioned and searchable.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes with these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Space" icon="plus">
    Sign up at `https://arvindgaba.com` and create a new documentation space for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-text">
    Click "New Page" and start writing in Markdown. Use the editor for rich formatting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize with Folders" icon="folder">
    Drag pages into folders or add tags like `api`, `guide` for quick filtering.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share and Collaborate" icon="share-2">
    Generate a share link or invite users via email. Set permissions for view or edit access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Pro tip: Use keyboard shortcuts like <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> for instant search.
</Callout>
