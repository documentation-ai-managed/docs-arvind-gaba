## Symptoms

- Clicking "Save" does nothing
- Page content not updated

## Possible Causes

- Lost network connection
- Session timed out

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check internet connection">
    ````bash
    ping arvindgaba.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the editor">
    Reload the page and log in again if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try saving again">
    Make small change and click "Save."
  </Step>
</Steps>
